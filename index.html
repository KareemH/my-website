Get to know me

Raised in a non-tech savvy household, my younger self always wondered why my computer was slow, what a "404 Error" was, and how to avoid the anxiety inducing error message noise of a Windows desktop. Without knowledge of RAM mechanics or how web technologies worked, I was left clueless. Of course, I was told to shut down the computer and wait 10 seconds (which did work sometimes!). However, this seemed like a naive solution to such a complex device, motivating me to investigate what really goes on under the hood. And as I matured, Computer Science has granted me a "behind the scenes" look to everything that used to confound me and I continue to exponentially grow interest in tech.

I am a detail-oriented and inquisitive programmer aiming to leverage an existing knowledge of technical development/design to successfully occupy a junior software developer role. I focus in the domains of general software and web/full-stack development. However, I enjoy exploring and researching different paradigms of technology (data science, OS, networking, etc.) and relating it back to the context of my work.
